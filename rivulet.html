Rivulet UI/UX

Creative direction, branding, UX and design for a premium liqueur's luxury brand. Following through on the positioning of the liqueur as a luxury good meant an emphasis on beauty, and even a bit of excess, with a sharp, clean design with parallax scrolling functionality.

Primarily sold in overseas markets, steeping the brand in American history was important to its appeal.
