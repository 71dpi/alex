WeThrive! Campaign Case Study

TL;DR
Alex art directed and designed various forms of media for an initiative promoting healthier foods in schools, community gardens and safe parks for children to play in Hamilton County, Ohio. The work garnered 63 million outdoor impressions, 2.7 million display impressions, 140% increase in Facebook fans over 6 months, 129% increase in Twitter followers over 6 months, and won multiple awards.

Marketing campaign for the Hamilton County Public Health board and part of Michelle Obama's "Let's Move!" childhood wellness initiative. Deliverables included an award-winning Digital Out-of-Home experience using Microsoft Kinect technology, a website, billboards, flyers, award-winning poster series, and more.
