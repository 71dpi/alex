WeThrive! Campaign Case Study

Marketing campaign for the Hamilton County Public Health board and part of Michelle Obama's "Let's Move!" childhood wellness initiative. Deliverables included an award-winning Digital Out-of-Home experience using Microsoft Kinect technology, a website, billboards, flyers, award-winning poster series, and more.
